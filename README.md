## 🎯 Research Focus

### Problem 1 — Informal Sector Exploitation

Documented in:

* British International Investment (2022)
* IFC Case Study (2023)

Challenges:

* Weighing fraud
* Delayed payments
* No audit trail
* Supply instability

Solution:

* LR Collection App
* Digital scale integration
* Biometric facial verification
* Instant digital payments
* GPS route optimisation
* Demand forecasting

Result:

* Capacity scaled from 22 TPD → 700+ TPD
* 78% pickers report improved quality of life (IFC 2023)
* 25% income premium vs informal sector

---

### Problem 2 — EPR Compliance Complexity (Post-2022 PWM Amendment)

Challenges:

* Regulatory velocity
* Certificate fraud risk
* Q4 compliance gap price spikes
* Multi-state, multi-category obligations

Solution:

* EPR Connect dashboard
* NLP regulatory parsing
* Compliance gap predictor (Linear Regression)
* AI-assisted advisory notes

Result:

* 16% revenue from EPR in first full year
* Coverage across 33 states & UTs
* Predictive model (R² = 0.595)
